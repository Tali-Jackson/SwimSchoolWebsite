What is Splish Splash Swim School About?
Our Learn to Swim program

Our program focuses on developing individuals to become the best swimmers possible. Starting young and building confidence through postives renforcmentng and development of a supportive network of coaches sets all of your pupils up for success.

We have developed 8 key principles for our classes, which our the foundation of our business and our pupils success

  • 1 Individualisation - all classes/programs are tailored to each pupil
  • 2 Variety - no lesson is the same
  • 3 Classes longer then 30mins
  • 4 Progression is based on skill not age
  • 5 Positive renforcment over consequences
  • 6 Good mental health = good physical health
  • 7 Continuous improvement
  • 8 Any one can learn to Swim
